Meb - Ebook Platform

A Bookstore for Eveyone

The Problem

Meb’s old app was a troubling experience for new users, who struggled to find books they wanted. Despite numerous usability issues, Meb feared that a redesign might cost them their best customers.

The Goal

How might we make it easier for new users to find and read books they love while convincing avid readers that the Meb app they love is still here but better?

Our Process

Fuelled by user research, our design process blends design thinking, lean UX methodology, and agile to design and iterate solutions that solve real user problems.
