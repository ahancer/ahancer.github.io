Thai Revenue Department

One portal to access all tax services

Thai Revenue Department (RD) wanted to improve their services, by combining all of their services in one online channel. Our job was to organize their online services so that individual tax payers can easily find the services that they need.

Background

“When I enter the RD website, I don’t know where to start. Their services are indistinguishable.”

This is a common complain from Thai citizens when they need to file their taxes. The Revenue Department knows about this issue. However, to organize all their services and make easy to access from one touch point not a simple task.

The new website aims to help users access all services from one place and able to complete their tasks without leaving the website.

Handle all tax activities with one personal account
  • Users will be able to calculate, file, pay, and track their tax refund from one place.
  • Users will be able to complete activities, such as download forms and view their tax decutions, in one place.
Easy filing process with less technical terms
  • Users will find it easier to choose their correct tax type for filing if the description uses fewer tax jargons.
  • New users will be able to take a quiz to identify the type of tax form they should file.
Track all status in one place
  • Users will be able to track all filing status from all types in one place.
  • Users will be able to pay taxes and submit additional documentation all in one place.
