Ahancer Service

Discovery

At its core, our discovery process helps you reduce product risk. For some clients this might mean validating potential app concepts to see which direction to take. For others this might entail developing a better understanding of the current product’s usability issues.

While for others this might mean helping them to take an idea in their head and turn it into a tangible journey that will lay a strong foundation for their UI. The activities may vary but the objective is always the same; help shed light on your assumptions and prevent delayed customer validation through discovering the unique interplay between your users & business, content, and interaction.

Key Methods

User test
